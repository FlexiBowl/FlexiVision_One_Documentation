# **Warranty**

```{important}
   The full warranty terms are included in the **sales contract**.
   In the event of any discrepancy, the conditions established in the contract take absolute precedence over the content of this section.
```
---

## General Validity Conditions

For the warranty to remain valid, the following operating requirements must be met:

```{list-table}
   :widths: 30 70
   :header-rows: 1

   * - Scope
     - Mandatory Condition
   * - **Receipt**
     - Packages must be opened using suitable tools without damaging the system.
   * - **Commissioning**
     - Installation and start-up must be carried out by qualified and trained technicians.
   * - **Use**
     - The machine must be used within the contractual and technical limits specified.
   * - **Maintenance**
     - Maintenance must be performed on schedule using original **ARS S.r.l.** spare parts and authorized personnel.
```
---

## Warranty Exclusions and Forfeiture

The warranty is considered **null and void** if one or more of the following conditions occur:

```{warning}
   **Tampering and Safety**

   * Removal or tampering of safety devices (guards, sensors, microswitches).
   * Unauthorized modifications to the machine control software.
   * Tampering with electrical or pneumatic circuits.
   * Removal of the identification plate or safety pictograms.
```
```{error}
   **Improper Use and Negligence**

   * Failure to comply with safety regulations and manual instructions.
   * Use by untrained or unauthorized personnel.
   * Failure to carry out the scheduled maintenance plan.
   * Installation in unsuitable premises or faults in the power supply (electrical/pneumatic).
   * Modifications or repairs performed without written authorization from the Manufacturer.
```

```{note}
   The warranty also excludes damage resulting from exceptional events (floods, fires not caused by the machine) and disposal that does not comply with regulations.
```
